About Us


S & D Streetwear Market is a Streetwear marketplace for users to come, see, buy, and sell streetwear stuffs. S & D Streetwear Market established in July, 2018.

One of the reason that S & D Streetwear Market established is to make sure that people have a safe place to make a transaction, and get a guarantee that the item received is Authentic and brand new. One of the other reason is to gather people all around Indonesia to expand the culture of streetwear.

Our motto is to be the biggest Streetwear Marketplace in Indonesia.

Our way of work is very simple, yet safe. It just need a little effort from all of the users and admins to use our service.
